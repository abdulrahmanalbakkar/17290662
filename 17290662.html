Yabancı Öğrenci Sınavı

The YÖS Examination is an entrance examination designed for foreign students wishing to study in higher education institutions in Turkey. This examination was set and administered by the Student Selection and Placement Centre until 2010, when universities began conducting their own versions of the exam.

Scholastic Assessment Test

The SAT is a multiple-choice, pencil-and-paper test created and administered by the College Board. The purpose of the SAT is to measure a high school student's readiness for college, and provide colleges with one common data point that can be used to compare all applicants.
